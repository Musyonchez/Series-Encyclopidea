The Bad Batch Cast and Characters

Previous Next

Starring


Dee Bradley Baker as the Bad Batch

A squad of elite clone troopers also known as Clone Force 99, consisting of Hunter, Wrecker, Tech, Crosshair, and Echo.Star Wars creator George Lucas wanted the Bad Batch to be more unique than other clones, with each having special abilities, but he did not want them to be superheroes. Baker also voices the other clones in the series, including Cut Lawquane and Captain Rex.

Archie Panjabi as Depa Billaba

A Jedi Master to Caleb Dume

Michelle Ang as Omega

A young female clone working as a medical assistant on Kamino. She too is genetically deviant from standard clone templates and therefore feels a kind of kinship with the Bad Batch.
